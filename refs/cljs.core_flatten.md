Takes any nested combination of sequential things (lists, vectors, etc.) and
returns their contents as a single, flat sequence.

`(flatten nil)` returns nil.

```clj
(defn flatten
  [x]
  (filter #(not (sequential? %))
          (rest (tree-seq sequential? seq x))))
```
