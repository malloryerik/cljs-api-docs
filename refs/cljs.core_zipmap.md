```clj
(defn zipmap
  [keys vals]
    (loop [map (transient {})
           ks (seq keys)
           vs (seq vals)]
      (if (and ks vs)
        (recur (assoc! map (first ks) (first vs))
               (next ks)
               (next vs))
        (persistent! map))))
```
