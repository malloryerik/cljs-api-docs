When `test` is not nil, evaluates `body` with `x` bound to the value of `test`.

```clj
(defmacro when-some
  [bindings & body]
  (assert-args
     (vector? bindings) "a vector for its binding"
     (= 2 (count bindings)) "exactly 2 forms in binding vector")
   (let [form (bindings 0) tst (bindings 1)]
    `(let [temp# ~tst]
       (if (nil? temp#)
         nil
         (let [~form temp#]
           ~@body)))))
```
