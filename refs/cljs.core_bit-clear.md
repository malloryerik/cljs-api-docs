 <samp>
(__bit-clear__ x n)<br>
</samp>

---

Clear bit at index `n`.  Same as `x & ~(1 << y)` in JavaScript.

---

###### Examples:

Bits can be entered using radix notation:

```clj
(bit-clear 2r1111 2)
;;=> 11
;; 11 = 2r1011
```

Same numbers in decimal:

```clj
(bit-clear 15 2)
;;=> 11
```
