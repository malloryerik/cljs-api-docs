`'foo` is sugar for [`(quote foo)`](special_quote.md).

Prevent the evaluation of the following form.

---

###### Examples:

```clj
'foo
;;=> foo

'(a b c)
;;=> (a b c)

'[a b c]
;;=> [a b c]

'(a b (c d))
;;=> (a b (c d))
```
