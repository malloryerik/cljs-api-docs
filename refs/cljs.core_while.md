 <samp>
(__while__ test & body)<br>
</samp>

---

Repeatedly executes `body` while `test` expression is true. Presumes some
side-effect will cause `test` to become false or nil.

Returns nil.

```clj
(defmacro while
  [test & body]
  `(loop []
     (when ~test
       ~@body
       (recur))))
```
